About Lochbot

Lochbot is a free, browser-based chatbot response tester designed for developers, product managers, and anyone building conversational interfaces. It lets you experiment with system prompts and see how different configurations affect chatbot behavior.

How It Works

The tool uses rule-based heuristics rather than actual AI. It analyzes your system prompt for tone indicators, keywords, and structural patterns, then generates responses that follow common chatbot behavior models: greetings, clarifying questions, problem-solving flows, and conversation closings. Each turn is scored on three dimensions: relevance, tone match, and response length appropriateness.

This approach is intentional. By using predictable heuristics, you can clearly see how different prompt phrasings lead to different response patterns without the variability of a live language model.

Who This Is For

  • Developers prototyping chatbot system prompts before deploying to production
  • Product managers evaluating chatbot tone and response quality
  • Students learning about conversational AI design patterns
  • QA teams building test cases for chatbot interactions
